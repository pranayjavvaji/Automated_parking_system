How to use:

  1. Enter your SmartPSS URL and channel number
  2. Click "Capture Frame" to get an image from the RTSP stream
  3. Click and drag on the image to define parking spaces (rectangles)
  4. Each rectangle will auto-save when you release the mouse
  5. Click ✏️ icon to edit space names
  6. Use "Save to Database" to store configuration permanently
  7. Use "Load from Database" to retrieve saved configurations
  8. Export coordinates for your ML model

Sample SmartPSS URLs:

  • http://127.0.0.1:45132/?rtspport=45136&tcpport=45128
  • http://127.0.0.1:28731/?rtspport=28735&tcpport=28727
